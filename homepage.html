What is a Data Science Paradox?

Data Science combines the practices of statistics, mathematics and data analysis to aid decision making in business. It is driven by data
and if this data is biased, inaccurate or incomplete then the forthcoming analysis will be negatively affected. A Data Science Paradox is a statistical result that differs from expectations, despite starting from a true premise. They are vitally important in identifying the cause of misleading and inaccurate results within data sets.


How do they affect Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML)?

Data Science paradoxes can play a huge part in negatively affecting AI and ML models, as they are trained on and create knowledge from such data which could be distorted by paradoxal information. This could lead to the models generating incorrect information which could potentially be harmful or dangerous to the model users. This ineffective training could also lead to higher energy consumption which is harmful to our environment.


What can we do to prevent paradoxes from occuring?

A paradox could occur when conducting any kind of AI or ML training. There are many things that we can try to prevent them such as proper randomisation, ensuring data consistency and detailed analysis of subsets. You can learn all about this and more on this very website!
